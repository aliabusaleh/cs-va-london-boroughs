London Boroughs Dashboard

This dashboard features interactive charts to display data from two London Datasets. All charts are clickable except for the four number displays at the top and the Obesity scatter plot. When a chart is clicked, filters are applied to other charts which will display results based on the selection. There is a reset all button at the bottom of the page to reset all charts.

Covered are some of the key indicators of any city such as demographic, economic, environmental and crime.

Most of the charts are based on a 2015 dataset found at The London Data Store.

The data used in the Crimes per Borough section was taken from a 2016 Met Police dataset.

A short tutorial is provided to run you through the best way to use the dashboard.

This information modal and the tutorial can also be accessed later via the icons at the top of the page.

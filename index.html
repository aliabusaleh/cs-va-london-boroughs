UK Immigration Employment Perception

This dashboard is desiged for People who are interested to immigrate to UK, to get a better understanding of the UK employment situation.

All charts are clickable except for the four number displays at the top. When a chart is clicked, filters are applied to other charts which will display results based on the selection. There is a reset all button at the bottom of the page to reset all charts.

Covered are some of the key indicators of any Region such as Population, Employment Population, Job Density, Level of Education, Occupation, Sectors, and Propotion of Self-Employed vs Employees.

Most of the charts are based on a 2016 dataset found at Office for National Statistics.

A short tutorial is provided to run you through the best way to use the dashboard.
